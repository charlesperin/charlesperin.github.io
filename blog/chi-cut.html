Posted 30 Sep 2015

As some have more information than others, it seems fair to me to share it as authors deserve to know the process. I do not certify the following formula is right, but here is what I heard:

You will get a 2AC if your paper fulfils one of the two following conditions:

  1. your average score is >= 3.0
  2. your average score is >= 2.5 AND your standard deviation is >= 1.3
